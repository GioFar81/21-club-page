Photo Walks around London

Meeting every months

Fun Photo Walks around Central London; we welcome any photographer levels, from beginner to professionals, and encourage people to socialize and help each other.

About Us.

Back in 2019 we started as a small group of friends with the same passion and slowly grown to a much bigger group throughout time. We started our online presence at the beginning of 2020 in Facebook and Instagram, and later on we created this website.

Our aim is to connect people with the same interest in photography and to create a community that help each other grow their passion no matter of their level of expertise and background.
